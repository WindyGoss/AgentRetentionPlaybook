Why Good Agents Leave: The Three Core Struggles

High turnover isn't random; it's a direct result of interconnected challenges that impact an agent's stability, growth, and well-being.

1. Financial Instability

The promise of large commissions often clashes with the reality of inconsistent income, especially for new agents. This "survival gap" is the leading cause of early-career attrition.

2. Inadequate Support & Training

A significant leadership gap exists in coaching and development. Without structured training and mentorship, agents feel overwhelmed, under-equipped, and are more likely to stagnate.

3. Suboptimal Team Culture

Poor communication, a lack of recognition, and unrealistic expectations erode loyalty. Burnout becomes inevitable when work-life balance is ignored, leading to disengagement.
